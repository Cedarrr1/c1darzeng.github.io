I am an undergraduate student in Computer Science and Technology at Central South University. My work focuses on data mining, time-series analysis, multimodal trustworthy intelligence, and large language model agents.

#### About Me

My research training started from time-series anomaly detection. I have worked on a vision-augmented framework that uses multi-view representations, prototype retrieval, and coordinated decoding to improve the modeling of temporal dynamics and structural patterns. This experience shaped my interest in problem-driven research, careful experimental validation, and interpretable model design.

I am now exploring how large language models can solve complex tasks more efficiently through adaptive reasoning, tool use, and multi-agent collaboration. I am especially interested in reasoning scheduling, dynamic communication, result verification, and trustworthy decision-making under realistic compute constraints.
